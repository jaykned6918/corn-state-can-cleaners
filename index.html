Still Have Questions? Check the FAQs!

Do I need to be home for the service?

Not at all! Just leave your bins at the curb on your scheduled service day, and we'll take care of everything. You can go about your day while we make your bins sparkle.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Council Bluffs, Omaha Metro, and surrounding neighborhoods in Iowa and Nebraska. We're expanding rapidly, so contact us if we don't serve your area yet!

Is your cleaning process safe for pets and children?

Absolutely! We use only eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Our process eliminates harmful bacteria without using harsh chemicals.

How do I pay for the service?

We accept all major credit cards through our secure Stripe payment system. Simply click "Sign Up!" and you'll be taken to our secure payment page to complete your subscription.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes! You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Just contact us at least 24 hours before your next scheduled service.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll come back and re-clean your bins at no charge, or provide a full refund.
